Welcome to Melody!

Melody Music School offers comprehensive musicianship through active learning for children and adults. Our music educators are highly qualified and share a love of teaching. Our curriculum offers group experiences, private lessons, ensemble participation, theater, and camps. We believe that learning music can be fun, and we believe in the effectiveness of learning in groups.
